About MECCA FARMS

At MECCA FARMS, we are passionate about providing innovative solutions to help households and students access cheaper food options by leveraging the power of group buying. Our mobile app connects users with farmers and wholesalers, allowing them to pool resources and make bulk purchases at discounted rates.

Our Mission

We aim to revolutionize food consumption by empowering communities to buy food conveniently, at a lower cost, while supporting local farmers and wholesalers.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Create an account and sign in to access group buying opportunities.

2. Browse Listings

Explore available food listings from wholesalers and farmers.

3. Join Group Buy

Join or create a group buy to pool resources and purchase in bulk.

4. Receive Your Order

Once the buy is complete, get your food delivered to a designated location.

Our Solution

We provide a mobile app that simplifies group formation, ordering, and payments, allowing users to buy food in bulk from farmers and wholesalers at a cheaper rate.

Save Money

Lower your food costs by participating in group buys.

Make Money

Farmers and wholesalers can sell in bulk, increasing their revenue.

Save Time

The app streamlines the buying process, making it quick and easy.
